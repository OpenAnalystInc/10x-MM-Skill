# Marketing Funnel Patterns

> **Used by**: Discovery Agent, Copywriting Agent, Build Agent
> **When**: Planning page sequences, structuring multi-step conversion paths, designing follow-up flows

---

## 6 Core Funnel Types

### 1. Lead Magnet Funnel

**Goal**: Capture email addresses in exchange for free value.
**Best for**: Building an audience, top-of-funnel awareness, list building.

**Page Sequence**:
```
Landing Page (opt-in) → Thank You Page → Email Nurture Sequence → Sales Page
```

| Page | Key Elements |
|------|-------------|
| **Landing Page** | Headline promising the lead magnet, 3-5 bullet benefits, email form, preview image |
| **Thank You Page** | Delivery confirmation, next steps, soft intro to paid offer, social share buttons |

**Conversion Benchmarks**:
- Opt-in rate: 20-40% (cold traffic), 40-60% (warm traffic)
- Email-to-sale conversion: 1-5%

---

### 2. Tripwire Funnel

**Goal**: Convert cold traffic with an irresistible low-cost offer, then upsell.
**Best for**: E-commerce, info products, SaaS with starter tiers.

**Page Sequence**:
```
Ad → Low-Ticket Offer Page → Order Form → Upsell Page 1 → Upsell Page 2 → Thank You
```

| Page | Key Elements |
|------|-------------|
| **Offer Page** | Irresistible price point ($1-$27), high perceived value, urgency, strong guarantee |
| **Order Form** | Minimal fields, order summary, security badges, bump offer checkbox |
| **Upsell Page 1** | Complementary product, "one-time offer" framing, yes/no buttons |
| **Upsell Page 2** | Downsell or different complementary offer |
| **Thank You** | Order confirmation, access instructions, community invite |

**Conversion Benchmarks**:
- Tripwire conversion: 5-15%
- Upsell take rate: 10-25%
- Bump offer take rate: 20-40%

---

### 3. Webinar Funnel

**Goal**: Educate and sell through live or recorded presentations.
**Best for**: High-ticket offers ($500+), coaching, B2B services, courses.

**Page Sequence**:
```
Registration Page → Confirmation Page → Reminder Emails → Webinar → Replay Page → Sales Page
```

| Page | Key Elements |
|------|-------------|
| **Registration Page** | Webinar title, date/time, 3 key takeaways, presenter bio, countdown |
| **Confirmation Page** | Calendar add links, "what to expect," pre-webinar resource |
| **Replay Page** | Recording embed, time-limited availability, CTA below video |
| **Sales Page** | Full offer details, bonuses, payment options, FAQ |

**Conversion Benchmarks**:
- Registration rate: 20-40%
- Show-up rate: 25-40%
- Webinar-to-sale conversion: 5-15%

---

### 4. Sales Letter Funnel

**Goal**: Convert through long-form persuasive copy.
**Best for**: Info products, courses, coaching, supplements, direct response offers.

**Page Sequence**:
```
Long-Form Sales Page → Checkout Page → Upsell → Thank You / Access Page
```

| Page | Key Elements |
|------|-------------|
| **Sales Page** | Problem agitation, story, solution reveal, benefits, proof, offer stack, guarantee, CTA, FAQ |
| **Checkout Page** | Order summary, payment options, security badges, guarantee reminder |
| **Upsell** | Complementary offer, one-click add |

**Page Structure (Sales Letter)**:
1. Attention-grabbing headline
2. Problem identification and agitation
3. Story (founder, customer, or origin)
4. Solution introduction
5. Benefits (not features)
6. Social proof and testimonials
7. The offer (stack the value)
8. Bonuses
9. Guarantee (risk reversal)
10. Price justification
11. CTA
12. FAQ and objection handling
13. Final CTA with urgency

**Conversion Benchmarks**:
- Cold traffic conversion: 0.5-2%
- Warm traffic conversion: 2-10%

---

### 5. Product Launch Funnel

**Goal**: Build anticipation and sell in a compressed window.
**Best for**: New products, courses, seasonal releases, limited editions.

**Page Sequence**:
```
Pre-Launch Sequence → Launch Page → Cart Open → Cart Close → Waitlist
```

| Phase | Pages & Content |
|-------|----------------|
| **Pre-Launch (7-14 days)** | Teaser page, email sequence building anticipation, behind-the-scenes content |
| **Launch Day** | Sales page goes live, launch email, social media blitz |
| **Cart Open (3-7 days)** | Sales page with countdown, daily emails with different angles |
| **Cart Close** | Final urgency emails, countdown timer on page, "doors closing" messaging |
| **Post-Close** | Waitlist page for next launch, redirect from sales page |

**Conversion Benchmarks**:
- Pre-launch list engagement: 30-50% open rate
- Launch conversion: 3-10% of list
- Cart-close day: 30-50% of total sales

---

### 6. Free Trial Funnel

**Goal**: Get users into the product, then convert to paid.
**Best for**: SaaS, tools, platforms, subscription services.

**Page Sequence**:
```
Landing Page → Signup → Onboarding → Activation → Trial Expiry → Conversion
```

| Page | Key Elements |
|------|-------------|
| **Landing Page** | Value prop, feature highlights, social proof, "Start Free Trial" CTA |
| **Signup Page** | Minimal fields, no credit card (or card with clear free period), instant access |
| **Onboarding** | Welcome screen, setup wizard, first-value milestone, checklist |
| **Activation** | Guide to "aha moment," tooltips, progress indicators |
| **Trial Expiry** | Upgrade prompt, feature comparison, special offer |

**Conversion Benchmarks**:
- Signup rate: 2-10% (no card), 1-5% (card required)
- Trial-to-paid: 15-25% (card upfront), 2-5% (no card)
- Activation-to-paid: 40-60% of activated users

## Email Sequence Frameworks

### Welcome Sequence (Post Opt-in, 5-7 Emails)

| Email | Timing | Purpose | Content Focus |
|-------|--------|---------|--------------|
| **1. Delivery** | Immediate | Deliver the lead magnet | Access link, quick-start tip, set expectations |
| **2. Value** | Day 2 | Provide quick win | Actionable tip related to lead magnet topic |
| **3. Story** | Day 4 | Build connection | Founder/customer story, relatable struggle |
| **4. Teach** | Day 6 | Demonstrate expertise | Case study or framework, subtle product mention |
| **5. Offer** | Day 8 | Present the solution | Bridge from problem to product, CTA to sales page |
| **6. Proof** | Day 10 | Overcome objections | Testimonials, results, FAQ answers |
| **7. Urgency** | Day 12 | Drive action | Deadline, bonus expiry, scarcity, final CTA |

### Cart Abandonment Sequence (3 Emails)

| Email | Timing | Focus |
|-------|--------|-------|
| **1. Reminder** | 1 hour | "You left something behind" + product image |
| **2. Objection** | 24 hours | Address top concern (price, trust, timing) + testimonial |
| **3. Urgency** | 48 hours | Last chance, bonus or discount, FOMO |

### Post-Purchase Sequence (3-5 Emails)

| Email | Timing | Focus |
|-------|--------|-------|
| **1. Confirmation** | Immediate | Order details, access instructions, what's next |
| **2. Quick Start** | Day 1 | How to get the most value immediately |
| **3. Check-in** | Day 3 | "How's it going?" + support resources |
| **4. Testimonial Ask** | Day 7 | Request a review or testimonial |
| **5. Cross-sell** | Day 14 | Related product or upgrade recommendation |

## Funnel Metrics to Track

### Primary Metrics

| Metric | Formula | Healthy Range |
|--------|---------|---------------|
| **Opt-in Rate** | Signups / Visitors | 20-40% |
| **Show-up Rate** | Attendees / Registrants | 25-40% |
| **Sales Conversion Rate** | Purchases / Visitors | 1-5% (cold), 5-15% (warm) |
| **Average Order Value (AOV)** | Revenue / Orders | Varies by offer |
| **Customer Lifetime Value (LTV)** | AOV x Purchase Frequency x Lifespan | 3-5x AOV target |

### Per-Stage Metrics

| Stage | Key Metrics |
|-------|------------|
| **Traffic** | Visitors, cost per click, click-through rate |
| **Capture** | Opt-in rate, cost per lead, lead quality score |
| **Nurture** | Email open rate (20-30%), click rate (2-5%), unsubscribe rate (<0.5%) |
| **Conversion** | Sales conversion rate, AOV, cart abandonment rate |
| **Retention** | Repeat purchase rate, churn rate, NPS score |

### Funnel Health Indicators
- **Healthy**: Each stage converts at or above benchmarks
- **Leaking at top**: Low opt-in rate → fix offer or targeting
- **Leaking at middle**: Low engagement → fix nurture content
- **Leaking at bottom**: Low conversion → fix offer, price, or trust

## Cross-Sell & Upsell Page Structure

### Upsell Page Template
```
1. "Wait! Your order is not complete..."
2. Acknowledge the purchase they just made
3. Introduce the complementary product
4. Explain why it pairs perfectly with their purchase
5. Stack the added value
6. Special "customer-only" price
7. One-click "Yes, Add This" button (no re-entering payment)
8. "No thanks, I'll pass" text link below
```

### Cross-Sell Section (On Thank You Page)
```
1. "Customers who bought [X] also love..."
2. 2-3 related product cards
3. Short benefit bullet per product
4. "Add to Order" buttons
5. Keep it brief — they already bought
```

### Downsell Strategy
If the upsell is declined, offer a smaller version:
- Full course declined → Offer just Module 1
- Annual plan declined → Offer monthly
- Premium add-on declined → Offer lite version at 50% the price
